# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

We are committed to making participation in this project a welcoming experience for everyone. We ask all contributors and community members to:

- **Be respectful and considerate** in all interactions, whether in issues, pull requests, or discussions.
- **Be constructive** when giving feedback. Focus on the idea, not the person.
- **Stay focused** on the project's goals and technical merit.
- **Accept responsibility** for mistakes and learn from them gracefully.
- **Follow the guidance** of project maintainers regarding contribution standards and project direction.

## Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness behavior that violates these standards, please contact the project maintainers. All reports will be reviewed and addressed appropriately.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards. They may remove, edit, or reject contributions that do not align with this code of conduct, and may restrict participation for individuals whose behavior is harmful to the community.
